about

Backend by trade, AI by recent necessity

I've spent most of my career on the parts of e-commerce platforms customers never see directly — catalog systems, search infrastructure, pricing pipelines, and the backend plumbing that keeps millions of products indexed, priced, and discoverable. I started on Oracle ATG and Endeca, led the migration of monolithic commerce systems to AWS-native microservices, and now lead catalog and AI/ML integration efforts for a fashion & home retail platform.

Over the last year, that's expanded into applied AI: fine-tuning classification models on AWS SageMaker, building LLM-based ranking pipelines with OpenAI's APIs, and — most recently — leading org-wide adoption of AI-assisted engineering tooling (Claude, Cursor, Copilot) that cut development cycle time by 80% across my team.

I care about systems that are boring in production — observable, well-instrumented, and easy to hand off — and I like mentoring engineers into being the kind of on-call partner you actually want at 2am.
